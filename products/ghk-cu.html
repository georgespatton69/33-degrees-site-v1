GHK-CU

Copper Peptide Research ·

Overview

GHK-CU is a naturally occurring copper peptide studied for mechanisms in collagen synthesis, hair follicle signaling, and wound repair pathways. GHK-CU plasma levels decline with age in biological systems, making it a subject of aging research.

Research Mechanisms

  • Investigated for mechanisms in collagen synthesis and skin remodeling
  • Research examines effects on hair follicle signaling pathways
  • Studied for wound repair and tissue regeneration mechanisms
  • Copper-peptide complex investigated for antioxidant properties
