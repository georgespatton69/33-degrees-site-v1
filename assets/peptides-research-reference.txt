WHAT ARE PEPTIDES?
------------------
A peptide is a short chain of amino acids — the same building blocks that make up proteins. Peptides act like biological signals or messengers that tell the body to do very specific things at the cellular level. Think of them like a text message between cells telling your body what to do.

Depending on the peptide, they can:
- Help with healing and tissue repair
- Support muscle growth
- Improve skin, collagen, and anti-aging
- Regulate hormones
- Assist with fat loss and metabolism
- Influence immune function

What makes peptides powerful:
- Targeted — they do one specific job
- Efficient — small and easily used by the body
- Natural — mimic what your body already produces


=== HEALING PEPTIDES ===

TISSUE REPAIR & REGENERATION
-----------------------------

BPC-157
- One of the most popular healing peptides
- Derived from a protein found in the stomach
- Known for: accelerating muscle/tendon/ligament repair, healing gut lining (ulcers, leaky gut), improving blood flow to injured areas
- Commonly used for: injuries, inflammation, gut health

TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)
- Works systemically (throughout the body)
- Promotes: cell migration and tissue regeneration, faster healing of muscles and soft tissue
- Often stacked with BPC-157 for injury recovery

ANTI-INFLAMMATORY & IMMUNE SUPPORT
------------------------------------

Thymosin Alpha-1 (TA1)
- Supports the immune system
- Helps reduce chronic inflammation
- Used for: immune regulation, viral recovery support, general healing support

RECOVERY, GROWTH & REPAIR
---------------------------

CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Stimulates natural growth hormone release
- Benefits: improved recovery and repair, better sleep (deep healing phase), increased collagen production
- More indirect healing (through GH pathway)

ADVANCED / EMERGING HEALING PEPTIDES
--------------------------------------

GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide)
- Powerful for: skin healing and anti-aging, collagen and hair growth, wound repair
- Often used topically or injected


=== PEPTIDES FOR MUSCLE GROWTH ===

How they work: 1) Increasing growth hormone, 2) Improving recovery + repair, 3) Enhancing nutrient partitioning / muscle building signals

GROWTH HORMONE–BOOSTING (Most Important)
------------------------------------------

CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- The gold standard stack for muscle growth
- Works by increasing your body's natural GH pulses
- Benefits: lean muscle gain, faster recovery, better sleep, fat loss alongside muscle gain
- Closest thing to "safe, sustainable" growth

MK-677 (Ibutamoren) — technically not a peptide, but used with them
- Oral GH secretagogue (mimics ghrelin)
- Benefits: increases GH + IGF-1, strong appetite boost (helps bulking), improves recovery and sleep
- Note: can cause water retention + increased hunger

MUSCLE REPAIR & PERFORMANCE
-----------------------------

BPC-157
- Not a direct muscle builder but critical
- Heals tendons, ligaments, and muscle tears
- Lets you train harder and more often — "injury-proofing" your gains

TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)
- Speeds up full-body recovery
- Benefits: faster muscle repair, reduced inflammation, increased mobility

ADVANCED MUSCLE GROWTH
-----------------------

IGF-1 LR3
- Directly stimulates muscle cell growth
- Benefits: increases muscle size (hyperplasia potential), nutrient uptake into muscle cells
- Note: much stronger, requires more precision and caution

Follistatin (FST-344 / FST-315)
- Blocks myostatin (the protein that limits muscle growth)
- Benefits: potential for rapid muscle gain, breaks natural growth limits
- Note: very advanced / experimental

SIMPLE STACK STRATEGY
- Beginner/safest: CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin + good diet + training
- Performance-focused: Add BPC-157 (injury prevention)
- Advanced: IGF-1 LR3 or MK-677 added


=== PEPTIDES FOR ENERGY ===

DAILY ENERGY & VITALITY
-------------------------

CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Increases growth hormone → improves deep sleep, daytime energy, overall vitality
- Most people feel more energized because they're finally recovering properly

Tesamorelin
- Stronger GH-releasing peptide
- Benefits: boosts metabolism, reduces visceral fat (which drains energy), improves mental clarity

MENTAL ENERGY & FOCUS
----------------------

Semax
- Nootropic peptide (brain-focused)
- Effects: increased focus and mental clarity, reduced brain fog, mild stimulant-like effect (without crash)
- One of the best for clean, productive energy

Selank
- Calms anxiety while improving cognition
- Why it helps energy: less mental stress = more usable energy, smooth stable mood

CELLULAR ENERGY & ANTI-FATIGUE
--------------------------------

MOTS-c
- Works at the mitochondrial level (your cells' energy factories)
- Benefits: increased endurance, better metabolic efficiency, fatigue resistance
- One of the closest things to a true "energy peptide"

AOD-9604
- Helps with fat metabolism
- Energy impact: less sluggishness, improved metabolic energy

ENERGY DECISION GUIDE:
- Tired from poor recovery → CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Want mental clarity + focus → Semax (+ Selank if stressed)
- Want physical stamina + endurance → MOTS-c
- Slow metabolism / low drive → Tesamorelin or AOD-9604

Best overall energy upgrade: CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
Best for focus/productivity: Semax
Best for physical energy/endurance: MOTS-c


=== PEPTIDES FOR WEIGHT LOSS ===

GLP-1 PEPTIDES — #1 by far
----------------------------

Semaglutide (Wegovy / Ozempic)
Tirzepatide (Zepbound / Mounjaro)
- Kill appetite, slow digestion → stay full longer, improve blood sugar + insulin
- Results: ~10–20% body weight loss in many cases
- Best for: people struggling with overeating, fast noticeable fat loss

FAT-BURNING (Metabolism Focus)
-------------------------------

AOD-9604
- Derived from growth hormone, targets fat burning directly (lipolysis)
- Helps reduce stubborn fat, doesn't affect blood sugar
- Mild results compared to GLP-1s

Tesamorelin
- Boosts natural growth hormone
- Reduces visceral (belly) fat, improves body composition
- Especially good for stubborn abdominal fat

BODY RECOMPOSITION
-------------------

CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Burns fat slowly, preserves or builds muscle
- More of a lean, toned look vs rapid weight loss

ADVANCED METABOLIC
-------------------

MOTS-c
- Works at the cellular (mitochondria) level
- Improves metabolism, increases energy + fat utilization
- Still emerging but promising

WEIGHT LOSS DECISION GUIDE:
- Appetite/cravings → GLP-1 (Semaglutide or Tirzepatide)
- Already lean, stubborn fat → AOD-9604 or Tesamorelin
- Muscle + fat loss together → CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Metabolic optimization → MOTS-c


=== PEPTIDES FOR MENTAL CLARITY ===

Semax — #1 for Focus & Cognitive Performance
- Boosts focus, learning, and memory
- Increases BDNF (brain growth & repair factor)
- Supports dopamine & serotonin balance
- Feels: clean sharp focus, no jittery stimulant effect, improved mental stamina
- Widely considered the strongest peptide for mental clarity

Selank — Calm, Clear Thinking
- Reduces anxiety (huge for brain fog)
- Improves focus under stress
- Modulates GABA, serotonin, dopamine
- Feels: calm but alert, less mental chatter, easier to concentrate
- Many users report clearer thinking within 1–2 weeks

Semax + Selank Stack (Most Popular Combo)
- Semax → increases focus + brain performance
- Selank → removes anxiety + mental noise
- Together: focus, memory, stress resilience, mental clarity
- Targets both cognition + emotional balance

CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin (Indirect Brain Clarity)
- Not a direct nootropic but important
- Improves deep sleep, enhances recovery, boosts overall brain function
- Better sleep = better mental clarity the next day

MENTAL CLARITY DECISION GUIDE:
- Sharp focus / productivity → Semax
- Brain fog from stress/anxiety → Selank
- Want both (best option) → Semax + Selank stack
- Poor sleep / burnout → Add CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- These are not FDA-approved for cognitive enhancement in the U.S.
- Long-term data is still limited
- Effects are subtle but real, not like Adderall
