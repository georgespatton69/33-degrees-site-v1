Our Commitment to Purity

Every batch of every compound is independently tested by accredited third-party laboratories before being made available. We publish full Certificates of Analysis (COA) so researchers can verify purity, identity, and quality for every product they receive.

1

Batch Received

Raw compound arrives from our manufacturing partners and is logged with a unique batch number.

2

Sent for Testing

Samples are sent to an independent, accredited laboratory for identity confirmation and purity analysis.

3

Results Published

Lab results and the full COA are published here and linked on each product page. Every unit ships with its batch number.
